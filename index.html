How the figures are calculated

Headcount cards are live (not affected by the date range). Utilisation, cost and project counts are driven by the From–To range.

Building blocks
  • Capacity = weekly capacity (default 45 h/wk; JSE 55 h/wk × project multiplier) × weeks in that month.
  • Weeks-per-month follow a fixed pattern, so 5-week months have more capacity than 4-week months.
  • Hours come from each project’s weekly entries; leave, management and public holidays are tracked separately per person.
  • If an engineer joined after / left before a month, their figures for that month are 0.
Avg Utilisation

Includes project + leave + management + holiday hours (true workload).

monthly% = (proj + leave + mgmt + holiday) ÷ capacity × 100
team% = average across engineers in scope
card = average of monthly team% over the range
Billable Util

Project hours only (excludes leave, management, holidays) — always ≤ Avg Utilisation.

monthly% = projectHours ÷ capacity × 100  (then averaged)
Est. Cost — a total, not an average
rate = per-engineer override → else BIM rate → else grade × office table
engineer cost = (sum of project hours over range) × rate
card = Σ engineer cost across scope

Rates vary by grade and office and are managed under Cost Rates. Wider range → higher cost.

Engineers (MEP) / BIM Team

Live headcount of the scoped team. Not date-based. The ring shows that group’s share of the scoped team.

Active Projects

Projects the scoped team booked any hours on within the range, of the global total (e.g. 14). Ring = used ÷ total.
